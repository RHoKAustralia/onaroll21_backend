• How do I roll?

    To play On A Roll 21™ is super easy. We've set out some main points for you to follow here: How to play. The rest is up to you! But if you have any issues or need guidance please create a support ticket using the button at the bottom of this page.

  • My dice is stuck. What to do next?

    The dice feature in On A Roll 21™ operates using a series of javascript and html code interactions that may be sensitive to rapid button clicking or tapping, or internet signal fluctuations. If this happens, simply log out or exit the browser window and log back in.

  • Can On A Roll 21™ run on mobile device?

    We are currently building an app version of On A Roll 21™, however you can save or bookmark this website on your preferred device like an app so you can conveniently use on the run or after taking a photo. In iPhone, visit onaroll21.com/app using your Safari browser, select the browser icon in the lower-centre of window (this will bring up your sharing options), and select Add to homescreen. Chrome and Android browsers do not currently support home screen launch, but do support bookmarking. Follow the steps outlined in this link.

  • Can On A Roll 21™ run on Blackberry?

    On A Roll 21™ is still being tested on Blackberry operating systems and some features may not work. We strongly recommend using chrome and an alternative smartphone device or desktop.

  • Can I post video files?

    Not at the moment. We've temporarily removed this while working on other functionality of the app.

  • Can I post more than one image?

    Only one attachment per post is supported for the moment.

  • Can I invite a friend to participate?

    Currently On A Roll 21™ is only available under licence in consultation with the On A Roll 21™ team.

  • If I'm going away during the 21 days?

    As reporting data is anonymously tracked in team participation over the 21 days, we ask that you notify the appropriate person at your organisation.

  • Can I share a post on Facebook?

    In the interests of privacy, social sharing has not been activated for this edition of On A Roll 21™.

  • What happens if I skip a day?

    Skipping a day and then catching up later is entirely at your discretion. You can accomplish multiple missions in a day if you need to. You will not be penalised nor will your experience be compromised in any way if you do not post each day. However, to get the most out of the behaviour change benefits of the product, we recommend participating as routinely and consistently throughout as you can. Setting a calendar reminder either first thing in the morning or at the end of your work day (when you're ready to post) is helpful.

  • How can I continue rolling after 21 days?

    Currently On A Roll 21™ is only available under licence in consultation with the On A Roll 21™ team. If you wish to extend your participation period, please contact us via email on support@onaroll21.com

  • I don't know how to do my mission?

    Be as creative as you like when interpreting the On A Roll 21™ missions. There are no correct ways of responding. Don't feel restricted by the need to post a photo or respond literally to the mission instruction in text form.  

  • What happens to the survey results?

    The survey responses at the beginning and end of the program, along with your mood map information, are anonymously aggregated and averaged to produce a group wellbeing result. This may be presented to a representative at your organisation and may be collated as part of wider research into On A Roll 21™ participation. In either case, no individual will be identified in the information gathered. For more on how we handle information, please visit our privacy policy.
